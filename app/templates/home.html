First Take On Ember

As web applications are expected to become more complex and handle more user interactivity, jQuery will not suffice. I've been playing around with Angular.js to some extent, but I'd also like to see one popular alternative, Ember.js.

I decided give Ember a shot by creating Psalmist, a simple web app. It’s surprisingly much more opinionated about everything, and forces clear separation of concerns between components.

University Project: Android App

KIT: Keep In Touch

KIT (Keep-In-Touch) is social emergency alert system for mobile devices. Essentially a simple social network, KIT is capable of broadcasting and receiving both daily updates and emergency help requests to relatives. Devices communicate through a back-end server.

It is written in Android-native Java, and tested with Mockito and Robotium.
